I want series support — the ability to link multiple books into a series with a shared story bible, character registry, and timeline that persists across volumes. Right now each book is completely isolated. If I'm writing a trilogy, I have to manually ensure character details, world rules, and timeline consistency across three separate projects.

A "series" should be a lightweight container that groups books in order and provides a shared `series-bible.md` (or JSON) that gets loaded into agent context for every book in the series. When Verity is drafting Book 2, she should know what happened in Book 1 without me pasting summaries into every conversation. When Sable is copy-editing Book 3, she should flag if a character's eye color contradicts Book 1.

The sidebar could show series as collapsible groups above the book selector. Creating a series should be as simple as selecting existing books and putting them in order. The shared bible should be editable from any book in the series.

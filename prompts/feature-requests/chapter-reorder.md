I want to be able to reorder chapters by dragging them in the sidebar or files view. Right now chapters are numbered directories (01-slug, 02-slug) and restructuring means manually renaming folders on disk. During revision, I frequently want to move a chapter earlier or later, merge two chapters, or insert a new chapter between existing ones.

Reordering should rename the chapter directories to maintain the numeric order, and ideally update the scene outline to reflect the new structure. If the scene outline references chapter numbers, those references should be updated too. This is a common operation during the revision phase — Forge often suggests restructuring and Verity needs the chapters in the right order to maintain narrative flow.

A drag handle in the chapters panel of the structured browser would be the most intuitive UX.

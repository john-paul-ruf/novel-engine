After a major revision cycle (e.g., Forge's structural feedback → Verity's rewrites), I want to see the impact of all changes in one view. A side-by-side comparison of the pre-revision and post-revision manuscript would show exactly what moved, what was cut, and what was rewritten.

This could live in BuildView or as a dedicated view. Select two snapshots (e.g., "end of first-draft" vs. "after first-revision") and see a full manuscript diff with chapters color-coded: unchanged, added, removed, or modified. Optional: zoom into individual chapters for line-by-line comparison.

Useful for author confidence (seeing tangible progress), for explaining edits to beta readers, and for documenting what actually worked in the revision process.

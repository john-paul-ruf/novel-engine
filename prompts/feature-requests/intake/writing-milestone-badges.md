Beyond the writing-goals feature request (daily/weekly targets), add visual milestone badges to celebrate progress and motivate writers. Unlock badges for: first 1K words, first chapter complete, 10K words total, 50K words (NaNoWriMo!), first full draft, first completed revision cycle, manuscript to copy-edit, 100K words, final version published.

Show badge progress in the sidebar or a dedicated "Milestones" section. Each badge has a small icon and name (e.g., "Sprint Starter: 1K words," "Novelist: 100K words"). Share-worthy on social media. Not gamified or annoying — just a clean visual record of achievement.

Writers respond to concrete milestones. This is the difference between "you've written 47,200 words" and "you're past the 50K threshold — you're a novelist."

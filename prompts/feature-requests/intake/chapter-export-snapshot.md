Sometimes you want to export a single chapter or a chapter range as a polished DOCX for feedback, without rebuilding the entire 90K manuscript. Today you have to either export the whole book or manually copy-paste chapters.

A quick export button in the FilesView chapter toolbar should let you: select one chapter or a range, choose output format (DOCX, EPUB, PDF, Markdown), and generate instantly. The export uses the same Pandoc pipeline as the full build, so formatting is consistent.

Especially useful for sharing work-in-progress with beta readers ("here's Chapter 5-8 for feedback") or for extracting a short story that started as a novel chapter.
